TaskAgent Premium Subscription Terms

Effective date: April 16, 2026

English

TaskAgent Premium is an auto-renewable subscription for advanced task management, unlimited task areas, cloud sync, deeper progress insights, and priority support.

  • Monthly plan: USD $5 per month.
  • Yearly plan: USD $49.99 per year.
  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.
  • The subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • Your account may be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions from your App Store account settings: Manage Subscriptions.
  • Any unused portion of a free trial, if offered, may be forfeited when you purchase a subscription.
  • Prices may vary by country and may be converted to local currency by Apple.

Related documents: Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

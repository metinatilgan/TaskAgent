Privacy Policy

TaskAgent Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

English

TaskAgent helps users manage tasks, daily routines, categories, reminders, profile preferences, and optional Premium status. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, Firebase user ID, display name, and profile preferences.
  • User content: tasks, notes, subtasks, categories, completion history, avatar images, and task attachments that you add to the app.
  • Subscription information: Premium plan state and purchase status used to unlock paid features.
  • Device permissions: notifications for task reminders, photo library access for avatars, and document picker access for attachments.

How we use information

  • To provide task management, reminders, cloud sync, profile settings, and Premium features.
  • To authenticate users and protect each user's data from other users.
  • To respond to support, privacy, or account deletion requests.

Third-party services

TaskAgent uses Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage. These services are provided by Google Firebase and process account data, app data, and uploaded files so the app can sync across devices.

Data sharing and sale

TaskAgent does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising SDKs. Data is shared with Firebase only as needed to provide authentication, database, and storage functionality.

Retention and deletion

We keep account and task data while your account is active. You can request deletion through the Account Deletion page. Some records may be retained for a limited time when required for security, fraud prevention, billing records, or legal obligations.

Children

TaskAgent is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact support@taskagent.app.

Üçüncü taraf servisler
